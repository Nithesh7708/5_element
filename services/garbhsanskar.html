  • What is Garbh Sanskar?
  • Garbhsanskar is a term derived from Sanskrit, where "garbh" means womb, and "sanskar" refers to the impressions or imprints. It is a set of practices, rituals, and techniques that are believed to have a positive influence on the overall development and well-being of a fetus during pregnancy. According to ancient Indian scriptures, the process of instilling virtues and values in an individual through repetitive good deeds is called 'Sanskar'. These actions become ingrained in one's nature.
